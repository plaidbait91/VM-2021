Penniless Princes strike again: Illegal businesses exposed.

Criminals caught smuggling juvenile implants into Sector 3.

Lebanon:On Thursday evening, a gang of smugglers were caught by the Border Security Sensors of Sector 3, trying to smuggle implants into the mainland from the waterways of Sector 6. To escape identification by the Sensors, the accused had removed their Bar-Code eyes, but were caught when one of them accidentally brushed his Humerus-Bar against a Security Sensor’s pole.

The smugglers were interrogated at the Law Enforcement Centre of Coastal Division. The records at LEC, Coastal Division showed that the accused had gone to the dangerous extent of removing their Memory-extension Chips in order to keep their secret information safe. In fear of facing the Truth-Pedestals, the gang members self-destructed themselves, using their stomach acids to blast their Circulatory Centre. However, as we all know, the only way to permanently remove the body information is by permanent elimination, as done in the Underworld. Brain-Probes have found that the smugglers belong to the group of activists who call themselves the ‘Penniless Princes’. This information was verified upon interrogating the illegal Street Surgeons near the coast, who confessed to having removed the criminals’ Bar-Code eyes. The Street Surgeons were subsequently arrested. The smuggled goods were found to be juvenile implants worth a sum of seventy-five erdas in Sector 3, but were apparently bought in Sector 6 for a mere sum of twenty-five erdas. The gang had been headed towards the Class three Incubators, perhaps to use the implants on the babies there and smuggle them into the Underworld. The citizens of Worker Division confessed to having collectively debited nearly fifty erdas to the Penniless Princes in the form of gift transfers previously, since they were promised mandatory juvenile implants for their children at a lower price.

While a large section of the population condemns the smugglers and their illegal actions, the Social Activists are raising their voices in support of the Penniless Princes, calling them the heroes of the poor. Not only that, it is the belief of many that these illegal implants are blessings in disguise for the residents of the Underworld, protecting them from the tyranny of DevCorp. Struggling under the pressure of necessarily taking the juvenile implants and the high costs of good quality implants, the working class have been requesting concessions. However, the cost of manufacturing has been withholding those concerned from taking the decision to waive the expenses for those who are in need.

The developers of these implants insist that the implants are in no way a necessity to their living, but the leaders of the working community say otherwise. ‘The only reason that the government hasn’t been making efforts to reduce the costs for the poor is because they are too involved in pleasing the rich kings out there,’ the President of Worker Division stated angrily to the Report Recorders. ‘We all pay nearly five erdas per household every month. We have the receipts too. We are ready to show them whenever you want us to. Why are we not considered as humans?’ One might even sympathise with the workers Underground, whose worth is measured in terms of how much of them is machinery!
