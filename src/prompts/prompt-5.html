Mass Murder Rocks Underworld Residents

Safety, morality and the meaning of God questioned after a chilling murder mystery is solved.

Underworld: About an year ago, when a young woman (in seemingly perfect health and with no known enemies) went missing soon after she left her friend’s home, her family tried to track her down and were informed that she had been permanently eliminated. But imagine the shock the already grieving family met when they found her brutally mauled dead body on the banks of River Wanha. This wasn’t a horrific event that was one of a kind, though. Soon after, there were fourteen other incidents of people who were young and in perfectly good health disappearing from the realm permanently and later being found mauled somewhere. Though people tried to believe that this was just the will of God, since the dates of death were on their calendars, nobody has been able to make any sense out of it. While it is common knowledge that people disappear permanently, events like these are seldom reported.

Disappearances are the norm in this realm as death is to those of us above. They always have been, in the Underworld, chalked down to the “workings of God”. But what is the meaning of God if he rips life away from a person in their twenties? That too in such a brutal manner? It means that God had nothing to do with it, especially if the result looks as messy as our past. It is murder, or rather a form of it, known in the Underworld only as part of their wildest myths. We have heard of the horrific murders of Nora and Jorek Ohail at their residence in Coastal Division. Killed brutally by their own bodyguards, this sweet and happy couple were found lifeless with all of their implants missing. Their bodyguards had been desperate for better quality implants and had taken the horrible step of murdering a rich couple for their costly implants. As gruesome as this news was when we first heard of it, a more chilling crime recently came to light when the LEC delved into the disappearances of the young people. This crime was carried out by a coldblooded serial-killer, Dia. Reported to be a maniac, but a very smart one, he had hacked into the Life Control Systems and painted each death as permanent elimination, finding out the dates were on their calendars. And then, he went after the victims and killed them with relish, merely out of bloodlust. As gut-wrenching as his crimes, or perhaps even more chilling is his cruel, manic laughter in the LEC cells when officials approach him. But a more chilling suspicion for those of us who dwell on the surface: was he really just one madman? Or are the citizens of the Underworld finally breaking because of DevCorp's control?
