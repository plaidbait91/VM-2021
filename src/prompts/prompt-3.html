A ruthless killer or a hero?

Member of Operation H claims innocent lives in the Underworld, yet to be apprehended.

It started about six months ago, when Alrik and Fille Jubree of Ambrew, Sector 5, Underworld, went missing soon after they left a social gathering at Ambrew Hall. Upon investigation, it was found that they had been ‘permanently eliminated’ from the Underworld. In other words, they were dissolved in acid and their remains were drained into the surface seas. Though this permanent elimination done by the Underworld on a regular basis is common news, and the citizens of the Underworld never try to track down these people, misinformed as they are, this incident was something that had made the headlines in the Underworld news channels for weeks on end. The reason: this elimination did not happen on any of the couple’s predestined dates.

‘Not a birthday, not an anniversary… nothing,’ said a close friend of the Jubrees. ‘The day is not there on their calendars at all. How could they be eliminated?’ That was a question everyone had. And the mysterious eliminations of sixteen other celebrities who were outspoken about the absurdity of the claims of the Middle Class Thinkers regarding the presence of humans above ground only strengthened the issue. After months of investigation, the Underworld authorities have found the reason behind the eliminations: someone had managed to hack into the Life Control Systems of the Underworld. Though the realm has named the man a cold-blooded serial killer, we know better. Unfortunately, he is our beloved Operative, Nefjet Wingh. Frustrated by how these influential members were countering our efforts of spreading the truth to the Underworld, he had decided to take matters into his own hands and went to the realm in disguise to kill them. Once caught by the LEC, Ambrew, he self-destructed himself. In order to save vital information regarding our world from being accessed by the realm, members of Operation H discretely dissolved his brain. While we as a clan grieve the loss of our brave-hearted young leader today, we must all remember and learn from the mistake he has made. As opposed as we are towards those of the Underworld, they are still our brethren. We wish to save them, not kill them. Every cause takes time to take effect and we must wait patiently for the day we see the Earth of our legends.
